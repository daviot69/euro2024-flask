Scoring


The following Points will be awarded for the Group Match Predictions:

  • Predicting the correct outcome (e.g. Home Win, Away Win, Draw) 15 Points
  • Predicting the correct Home Score 5 Points
  • Predicting the correct Away Score 5 Points
  • Bonus for a Perfect Prediction 5 Points

The following Points will be awarded for the Knock Out Phases:

  • For each correct Quarter Finalist 25 Points
  • For each correct Semi Finalist 50 Points
  • For each correct Finalist 75 Points
  • For correctly picking the Euro 2024 Winner100 Points

Picking a Wildcard Team will allow you to pick up extra Points for every Goal they score.

However, you will also be penalised for every Goal they concede.
